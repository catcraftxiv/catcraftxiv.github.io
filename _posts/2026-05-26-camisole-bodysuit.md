High waister one-pice top with many texture options and sizes

### 6.99 USD OR 10 USD TIER
> *Get the mod at [patreon] or [boosty]. Public after 26.08.2026!*

- Replaces: Southern Seas Swimsuit
- YAB & Rue S/M/L & Bibo+ M & Lavabod sizes by @CyrHatsuko (no buff)
- Dyeable
- 4 regular texture options + 3 sheer texture options

Compatible with both WC and SC legs (and Bibo legs for Bibo+ M size), and includes few options that might help to fit it with some bottoms, but can't fit just every

All Lavabod sizes done by [Cyr]! Lavabod frazetta is still in beta

Feel free to use `#catcraftxiv` tag on [twitter] and stuff! I will be glad seeing these :>
